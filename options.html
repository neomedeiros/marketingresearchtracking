Earnhoney for Chrome
Version: 1.47

What is navigation tracking?

As part of the Optin Network project, the EarnHoney plugin for Chrome will be the first of our DAPPs to carry our new Data Sharing feature (still on beta).
This means that the plugin will collect anonymous navigation data about your preferences as a consumer, as long as you are surfing the web with the tracking enabled.
We will NEVER collect personal data about you, like your name, location, contacts etc. Websites with sensitive information, including internet banking, adult content and private mail will be automatically ignored as well.

You can always turn the tracking on/off anytime manually.

What kind of data will be tracked?

  • Cookies
  • First click time
  • Screen resolution
  • Mouse positioning
  • Page clicks count
  • Date/time page request
  • Page indexing metatags
  • Total time spent on pages
  • Urls
  • Earnhoney identification
  • Total scrolling time
  • Public IP Address

What do I get from that?

As reward for participating in our beta program, will you receive bonus earnings when our app is officially launched.
When that happens, everyone engaged in the Optin Network will be rewarded in OPTins for sending consumer data to our servers.
Of course, beta testers get an extra depending on how much they have contributed. The number on the plugin shows how much OPTIN you have, and is updated every five minutes.

So keep sharing!
